Campaign Insights & Strategy

▲ Apr 10: Only 2 registrations and €80 revenue — the steepest single-day collapse. Check whether a campaign paused, a budget cap hit, or an ad was disapproved that day.
▲ Apr 4: 5 registrations vs 22 on Apr 2 and 20 on Apr 6 — mid-week trough. If spend was flat, your CTR or conversion rate dropped sharply. Worth segmenting by ad group for that date.
◆ Geo Distribution: DE + PT + IT + FR account for ~43% of registrants. Cross-reference with Meta Ads geo spend to find markets where you're underinvesting relative to conversion rate.
◆ Gender Mix: Gender is 64% male. If your creative/targeting isn't intentionally skewed male, your female audience may be underserved — or your ad creative self-selects. Worth A/B testing gendered creative.
